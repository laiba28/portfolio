About Me

Hey, I’m Laiba. A developer who loves turning messy problems into smooth, working systems. My journey started with frontend work (making websites look nice and easy to use), then I jumped into full‑stack (connecting the pretty stuff with the powerful stuff behind the scenes). And now… I’m all about automation. Think bots, scrapers, and big orchestration systems that can run thousands of devices without breaking a sweat.


I enjoy solving tricky edge cases like when jobs half‑finish, slots run out, or schedules get messy. I’m the kind of person who will refactor a database schema just to make sure everything is clean and future‑proof. My style? Persistent, curious, and a little obsessed with making things scale. Basically, if there’s a boring manual task slowing you down, I’ll find a way to automate it and make life easier.
